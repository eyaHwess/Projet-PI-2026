## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (> 992px)
- 4 stats cards in a row
- Full heatmap visible
- Monthly chart with all 12 bars

### Tablet (768px - 992px)
- 2 stats cards per row
- Heatmap scrollable horizontally
- Monthly chart with all bars (smaller)

### Mobile (< 768px)
- 1 stat card per row
- Heatmap scrollable horizontally
- Monthly chart scrollable

## Animations

1. **Card Hover**: Elevate with shadow
2. **Cell Hover**: Scale up 1.8x with border
3. **Bar Hover**: Translate up 5px
4. **Pulse Effect**: On productivity indicator
